简介 | Intro

嗨，我是yusi，一个爱玩的人，一个自由自在的人，一个兴趣爱好多到溢出的人。年轻的时候喜欢探索世界，依然年轻（？🤨）的当下喜欢探索内在。相信人生是旷野，是浪漫，是瞬间即永恒。相信世界是游乐场，是道场，是随时可抵达的远方。❤️

Hi, I'm Yusi, a playful individual, a free-spirited soul, and someone with an abundance of interests. In my younger days, I enjoyed exploring the world, and even now, in the present, I delight in exploring my inner self. I firmly believe that life is a wilderness, full of romance and moments that last forever. I perceive the world as a playground, a sanctuary, and a distant place that can be reached at any moment.
